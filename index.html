Privacy Policy — Matix (com.fourmatrix)

Last updated: November 30,2025 • App: Matix • Package: com.fourmatrix

Matix ("we", "our", "us") operates the Matix mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the App. By using Matix you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

We DO NOT collect personally identifiable information such as name, email, phone number, or contacts in the current version of the App. The App stores only the small set of data needed to provide core reminder & matrix features on your device.

a) Local device storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device (for example: SQLite database, files, or MMKV):

  • Tasks (title, description, category/quadrant)
  • Reminder details (date, time, repeat rules)
  • Categories and matrix metadata (Urgent, Important, etc.)
  • User preferences (theme, notification settings) in MMKV or similar local storage

All of the above data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

b) Temporary and backup files

The App may create temporary or backup files on your device (for example when exporting or backing up tasks). These files remain on the device unless you choose to export or share them.

2. Notifications & Scheduling

The App uses the device notification systems (AlarmManager, WorkManager, or platform scheduling) to trigger reminder alerts. We do not read or collect notification contents — the scheduling is local to your device.

3. No Account or Login

The current App version does not require account creation, email, or phone number. All data is available anonymously on the device.

4. Analytics & Ads

Current version: No analytics, no ads. We do not integrate Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, AdMob, or other third-party trackers in the current release.

If analytics or ad SDKs are added in a future version, we will update this Privacy Policy and disclose what data is collected and how it is used.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Since (in the current version) data stays on your device, we do not upload or share it with third parties.

6. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions (if and only if required by your Android version):

  • Notifications — to deliver scheduled reminders and alerts.
  • Storage (optional, older Android versions) — only if you export or import backups to device storage.

Permissions are used solely to enable the App’s features and are not used to collect personal data.

7. Data Security

Because your data is stored on the device, we have no direct access to it. To protect your data locally, follow standard device security practices (screen lock, backups). If you uninstall the App or clear app data, all stored tasks and preferences will be removed from the device.

8. Children

The App is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of the page. For significant changes (for example if we add analytics, ads, or remote backups) we will notify users via an in-app notice where possible.
